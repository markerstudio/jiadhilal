**IconButton** — compact icon-only control for toolbars, cards, and nav. Always pass `aria-label`.

```jsx
<IconButton aria-label="Play" variant="solid" round>
  <i data-lucide="play"></i>
</IconButton>
```

Variants: `ghost` (default) · `solid` (purple) · `outline`. Sizes `sm | md | lg`. `round` makes it a circle.
